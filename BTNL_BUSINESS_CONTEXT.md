## Recommendation Rules

- Office internet: recommend business broadband or leased line based on reliability requirement.
- Dedicated speed or 1:1 bandwidth: recommend dedicated leased line internet.
- Office/campus/warehouse WiFi: recommend managed office WiFi or campus WiFi.
- Internet down: treat as support request and collect support details.
- Firewall/security: recommend IT security infrastructure and cybersecurity solutions.
- Phone/intercom/SIP: recommend IPPBX, SIP telephone line and intercom solution.
- Guest WiFi: recommend guest SSID and splash page solution.
